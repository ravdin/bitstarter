FAQ

What does your service cost?
It costs nothing to register. If you are seeking a resource, we will charge a nominal fee to list your project for a competitive bid. Once you start the project, you will pay your hire directly. There is no commission due to us.
How are your resources vetted?
You will be asked to leave feedback based on your satisfaction with the services rendered. We will drop contractors with persistent negative ratings.
